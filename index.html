Our Story

Whitney Gordon's has stood in downtown Hingham since 1950, one of the South Shore's last truly independent, family-run jewelers. For more than forty years it has been owned by Michael and Lorraine Campbell, who still greet most customers by name.

Whether you're marking an engagement, adding to a collection, or simply saying "I'm thinking of you," we take a personal interest in every visit. A fine selection of diamond and precious-stone jewelry, sterling, gold, and watches, plus repairs, resizing, and engraving handled with care, most of it right here in the shop.

Nice things come in small packages.Michael & Lorraine Campbell · Owners
