Our Story

Hingham's jeweler
since 1950.

The Whitney Gordon's Jewelers has been a fixture in downtown Hingham Square for over 75 years. For the past 40 years, Michael and Lorraine Campbell have owned and operated the store — building relationships, not just making sales.

Whether you're looking for an engagement ring, a birthday gift, or a piece to mark a milestone, Michael and Lorraine take a personal interest in every customer's needs. It's the kind of service only a true local jeweler can offer.
