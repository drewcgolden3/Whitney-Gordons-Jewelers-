Our Story

Whitney Gordon's has stood in downtown Hingham since 1950 — one of the South Shore's last truly independent, family-run jewelers. For more than forty years it has been owned by Michael and Lorraine Campbell, who still greet most customers by name.

Whether you're marking an engagement, adding to a collection, or simply saying "I'm thinking of you," we take a personal interest in every visit. A fine selection of diamond and precious-stone jewelry, sterling, gold, and watches — and repairs, resizing, and engraving handled with care, most of it right here in the shop.

Repair · Custom · Engraving

A full-service jeweler, not just a counter

We repair and service most watches — batteries, bands, resizing — and bring heirloom and everyday pieces back to life: stone replacement, remounting, bead re-stringing, polishing, and engraving.

Have something new in mind? Bring in a few inspiration photos and we'll design a custom piece with you, from sketch to finished ring. If we can't do it in-house, we know someone who can.
