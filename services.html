What we do

Repair, restore, and create

We repair and service most watches (batteries, bands, resizing) and bring heirloom and everyday pieces back to life: stone replacement, remounting, bead re-stringing, polishing, and engraving.

Have something new in mind? Bring in a few inspiration photos and we'll design a custom piece with you, from sketch to finished ring. If we can't do it in-house, we know someone who can.
